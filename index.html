Hi, I am backer, a postgraduate psychologist. I have a great interest in researching fundamental questions, like where did we all come from? where are we going to? what is the real purpose of our life and so on. To these questions, there are answers basically from two perspectives. One is science and the other is religion. science tells a spontaneous origin story but religion has a different view of special creation.

In this age of science, the authenticity of anything is determined based on scientific research. So is it a fact that the universe came into being by chance and creationism is untrue? Is it scientifically proven? Or is it just a misinterpretation based on certain prejudices? These are important to understand those who want to set aside prejudices and other interests and understand things objectively. Such an analysis is intended here.
