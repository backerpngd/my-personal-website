ORBIT

All objects from fundamental particles to superclusters of galaxies are functioning under the guidance of the Creator. It is this divine system of creation that makes the peaceful existence of the universe possible. Like this, Islam is the complete way of life bestowed by the Creator to make human life immaculate and thereby achieve success in life. When a person organizes his life in accordance with divine guidance, he is called a Muslim. But many in the community are Muslims by name but do not pay enough attention to religious observance in life. Some even leave the religion. It is in this context that ORBIT - ORGANIZED RESEARCH BOARD OF AN INTELLECTUAL TEAM - was formed.

WHO ARE WE

It is a research group consisting of individuals working in multifarious academic domains who are interested in understanding Islam on the basis of authentic and proven scientific facts and living accordingly. The objective of this group is to provide proper religious education to the younger generation of the Muslim community, to cultivate in them an interest in higher education and research for the betterment of society, and to present the essence of Islam which has been misinterpreted by critics around the world. Headquartered at Kalpetta in Wayanad district, the venture is planning and implementing various projects to achieve its objectives.

WHAT WE DO

INTEGRATED RESEARCH PROGRAMME

ORBIT is conducting a research-oriented study project designed to nurture students at the degree and PG levels to develop a proper scientific and religious outlook and higher education in a way that is beneficial to them and the community. The first phase of the project is a two years long fundamental course prepared with a specially designed syllabus. Those who complete this course which is formulating a holistic outlook on life and research will have the opportunity to continue research training in socially significant subjects on the basis of religion and participate in ORBIT community initiatives.Read more...
